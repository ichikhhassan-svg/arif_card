Dear Arif, this is not how this was supposed to reach you at all. I had a card originally made here in Abu Dhabi, hand-written, and shipped over but the USPS choked on it when it was probably just a mile or two from your place. Anyways, this is the next best thing I can do it seems, so I hope it has a similar effect to what I had intended... It only seems like it was your last birthday last year, and although I wasn't able to be there to celebrate it, just like now, I still remember wanting to be there, perhaps one day we will get the chance to celebrate a birthday together isA. Although I have only known you just over a year, it seems like a lifetime. As we've said to each other multiple times, our connection seems to be one we had been searching for our entire lives. I can easily say for myself, that you are the friend that I had been looking for, and needing, since a very young age. And I think this sentiment allowed us to really develop a strong friendship so quickly. I cannot thank God enough for what's likely one of, if not the best friendships I have had, and I only ask that it continues for years and years to come. You have shown up in more ways than many I have known, and even continue to do so. You've also pushed me towards the right path, even when it may be difficult, and I have so much gratitude for that. People with such pure hearts aren't easy to come by, so I think it is very important to cherish them and hold them close when we do find them. I think without your presence, I certainly wouldn't be where I am today, especially considering all the positive life changes I have had within the past year, and for that, I will never be grateful enough.
Anyways, so let's get on to the presents I have for you. My main thought was to gift things that you would like, and need, but also things that can bridge the thousands of miles. I love gifting experiences, but since that might be hard, I thought that gifts which can allow us to have a similar experience would be best. To start, there's the Humantra boxes which you already opened. You mentioned liking them a lot, and I thought that they definitely have to be part of what you receive. I chose my personal favorite flavors, and really hope that you enjoy them as well. I would have loved to send some Dubai Chocolate to go along but considering weather and shipping, not the best idea. The second gift is a body spray I use occasionally. Here, I wanted to give a perfume or cologne of sorts, but I did struggle a bit with the scent which you would like. Then I remembered this one which I had, that fits right in that clean aquatic smell. I really hope that you enjoy it and it provokes some great sunny memories within. It is strong and lasts a long time on clothes, the kind I can put on before a BJJ session and still smell fresh afterwards! The last gift is something a bit closer to the heart for me. Last year, you introduced me to the idea of Tazkiyyah, which I hadn't really explored before much (or even heard about a lot). I would eventually end up on a whole series about it by ICPC, which you also introduced me to. Well, the series was based on this book, and I wanted you to have a copy for yourself. I hope that it will be a reference for you when needed, and always shine a light when things get dark like they tend to. So with that, I will end this letter and just hope that you enjoy all these little gifts, which don't really amount to how much I cherish you, my bestie, but may help express the sentiment. I hope that your day, week, month, and year are all filled with blessings, happiness, and love, and that we will cross paths again sooner than we think..
